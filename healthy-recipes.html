Healthy Recipes

"Diet" food doesn't have to taste bad.


We've all been there; you need to lose a few pounds but the thought of eating another salad almost tempts you into skipping dinner. There's only so much kale you can eat, after all! With so many fad diets out there, it's honestly amazing how many "healthy" recipes are bland (or worse).


A good diet has to be a lifestyle change rather than constantly counting calories or obsessing over exactly how many almonds you can eat before you're over your carb intake for the day. Focusing on eating whole foods, (the type of thing that doesn't need a nutrition fact label or only has one ingredient listed on it) and cooking with natural ingredients is a great start. In general, the less processed something is the better, but spices are definitely not off the menu! These wholesome recipes are so good that you'll think they're bad for you.
